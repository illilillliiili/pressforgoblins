definitely not three goblins in a trenchcoat

yes hello this is noah. please ignore the shifting height. nothing to see here. we i am the founder of an editorial outfit operating out of a dimly-lit, well-guarded workshop in london.

editorial goblin

sharpens what's already there. refines pacing, character arcs, tension, stakes, themes and emotion to make sure your story feels right. spots plot holes before they swallow your manuscript. handles the practical stuff: developmental editing, copyediting, line editing, proofreading and second reading.

creative goblin

brings fresh ideas, new angles and the momentum you need when you're stuck. plot twists, new characters, hooks and possibilities you never saw coming. might ask bizarre questions at you until something clicks. always curious, always enthusiastic.

marketing goblin

knows what agents and publishers want to see and what the market is hungry for. pitches, straplines, brand identity and positioning. makes projects impossible to ignore. his one-liners hit like a well-timed goblin brick.

the goblins are hungry for

• sci-fi
• fantasy
• horror
• romance
• speculative fiction
• graphic novels, comic books
• technical lore: board and card game guides, video game scripts, narrative design

the goblins cannot digest literary fiction (which often puts them into a deep sleep), poetry or non-fiction (unless it involves other fantastical creatures like them)

waypoints

character creation

a vibe check. you'll get to meet all three goblins. no gold required. they're a delight, obviously. tell us about your world, your ideas or the scene you can't stop thinking about. we'll see if we're the right fit – or if you should run while you still can.

exploring the world

you're at the ideas and brainstorming stage. the fun part before the real pain begins. so many possibilities! you're building the world, crafting the characters, deciding on the stakes and maybe even designing an inventive and memorable magic system. the creative goblin LOVES this stuff and will help you identify the ideas worth chasing.

levelling up

editorial development. you're writing... and writing... and writing. this is where many give up. but not you. the editorial goblin will provide you with an editorial letter detailing what works, what doesn't (and why) and a comprehensive action plan to get your book through to the other side.

upgrading your gear

manuscript assessment. your draft is complete. finally. you need an honest read from someone who knows the market and what agents and publishers are actually looking for.

bonus level

custom work. your project needs something bespoke, maybe from all three in the trenchcoat. tell us your weirdest requirements.

final boss

pitch time. your manuscript is ready. exciting, but not so fast. what is the one-liner? what are your comps? why should an agent care? the marketing goblin helps you nail the query package so you don't fail trying to storm the gates.

we quote based on manuscript length, timeline and complexity. start with a free vibe check and we can settle on a gold payment that works for you and the goblins. authors from a background traditionally underrepresented in publishing will receive a discounted rate.
